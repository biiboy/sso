PROCESS

1st - 2nd week of January

- Leader Send KPI Unit

- Leader send KPI Unit & Individual to subordinate

- Subordinate review KPI Unit & Individual

- Subordinate create KPI Individual

- All KPI complete & agreed

- Leader & Subordinate set discussion

- KPI doc send to Manager

Continuos Discussion

1st - 2nd week of June

- Leader & Subordinate discuss progress KPI for each Tactical Step

- Leader set Result & Comment

- Add New KPI (Optional)

- Leader & Subordinate complete & agree on Mid Review

- Update KPI doc Sent to Manager

Continuos Discussion

1st - 2nd week of December

- Leader & Subordinate discuss progress

- Leader set Result & Comment

- Subordinate do self Assessment

- Leader set final comment and result

- Leader & Subordinate complete & agreed

- Update KPI doc send to Manager
